Skin Knowledge Center

Learn about different skin conditions, their causes, symptoms and skincare tips.

Normal Skin

Well-balanced skin with adequate moisture, smooth texture and very few imperfections.

Dry Skin

Lacks moisture and often feels rough, flaky, tight and itchy.

Oily Skin

Produces excess oil that may clog pores and increase acne breakouts.

Acne

Caused by clogged pores, excess oil, bacteria and inflammation.

Combination Skin

Oily around the T-zone while dry or normal on the cheeks.

Sensitive Skin

Easily irritated by skincare products, weather and environmental factors.

Acne-Prone Skin

Skin that frequently develops pimples, blackheads and whiteheads.

Dehydrated Skin

Skin lacks water rather than oil, causing dullness and tightness

Hypermentation

Dark patches caused melanin productions

Eczema

A skin condition causing dry, itchy and inflamed skin.
